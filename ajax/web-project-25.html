World Highways Website Redesign

Project Info:

I redesigned three of Route One brand websites: World Highways, ITS International and Aggregate Business. All three of the sites have the same layout design with just the color stylings changed to represent each individual brand.

Once the design for the pages were approved, I created the assets and files the development team would need to complete this project.
