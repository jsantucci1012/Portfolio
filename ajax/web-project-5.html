Nimble Thinkers Website Redesign 2022

Project Info:

In fall of 2022, I redesigned, coded and maintained the Nimble Thinker's website. The company intended to do a full site redesign in the future but needed a temporary site in the meantime to update and modernize their look.

Project Details:

  • Company:Scranton Gillette Communications
  • Brand:Nimble Thinkers
  • Tools Used:Sublime Text, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • Date:October 2022 - December 2022
