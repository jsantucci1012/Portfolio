Easter Seals' Brochure

Project Info:

During my senior year in college, I took a class on the Current Topics in Marketing. Our professor had the class work with different businesses to gain real work experience and knowledge; my team chose to work with the Easter Seals in Joliet.

After meeting with the organization's director, we collected desired data for Easter Seals and presented our finding to their board. In addition to collecting data, we also created a proposal for the board to create a Young Professionals Advisory Board.

When creating our proposal, I took the initiative and created the brochure the board memebers could use to recruit new members to the advisory board.
