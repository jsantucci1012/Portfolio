PB Housing Giants Interactive Article

Project Info:

Pro Builder's annual Housing Giants report ranks the top U.S. home builders and looks at the top trends affecting the business of home building.

For the 2024 Giants report, the editors requested we create a custom interactive landing page to display all the data/graphics used in the report. Since the year's theme was "superheroes", it allowed me to take a fun and comical approch to the UI/UX design.

Project Details:

  • Company:Scranton Gillette Communications
  • Brand:Pro Builder
  • Tools Used:Adobe Photoshop and Adobe XD
  • Status:Due to a company acquisition, the project came to a halt.
  • Date:May 2024 - July 2024
