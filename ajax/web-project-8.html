TNAH 2025 Website

Project Info:

The New American Home® is the official show home of the annual International Builders’ Show®. Each year, a new home is unveiled to demonstrate the most noteworthy advancements in home building.

Since 2018, my job was to create the wireframe mockups for each new home. Once the designs and color scheme were approved, I created the assets and files the development team would need to complete the project.
