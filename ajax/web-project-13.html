PR Interactive Article

Project Info:

For the July/August 2023 issue of ProRemodeler, the editor's featured the mental health issues facing the construction industry.

Due to the seriousness of the article and its importance, the editors requested we create custom interactive landing pages to display all the data/graphics in an eye catching manor while still bringing attention to the issue.
